Extra Chill — Colors & Fonts

The Extra Chill colors and fonts, with a live preview of how they look in real interface pieces. Click any color to try a different shade and watch the whole page update. Use Light, Dark, or Auto to preview each mode — your edits are kept separately for light and dark and survive refreshes. When you like what you see, hit Export to download a snapshot you can open and send over. Everything stays in your browser; Reset clears the current mode.
